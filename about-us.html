OUR FOUNDATION

Built on reliability, accountability, and discipline

TC Van de Merwe Logistics is a South African private company (Pty) Ltd, VAT registered and built on a foundation of reliability, accountability, and disciplined operations. From our base in Midrand Glen, Gauteng, we serve businesses across provincial and national routes with transport solutions designed for consistency and long-term value.

Our ethos marries operational precision with a deep respect for the goods and roads entrusted to us — crafting logistics that are not simply about moving cargo, but about strengthening the commercial partnerships that depend on timely delivery.

After all, true logistics is more than transport; it is the quiet confidence of delivery, every time.
